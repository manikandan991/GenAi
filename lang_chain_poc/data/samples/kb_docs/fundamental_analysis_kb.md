# ✅ Fundamental Analysis Guide (For RAG / LLM Use)

## #1. Business Quality
- Consistent revenue growth (5–10 years)
- Stable/improving operating margins
- Competitive moat: brand, IP, distribution, scale, network
- Low cyclicality
- Clean governance & auditor reports
- Stable or rising promoter holding
- Zero promoter pledging

---

## #2. Profitability Metrics
**ROE**
- Good: > 15%
- Excellent: > 20%

**ROCE**
- Good: > 15%
- Excellent: > 20%

**Margins**
- Net Profit Margin stable or increasing
- Operating Margin improving
- EPS growth healthy

---

## #3. Growth Metrics
- Sales Growth (5Y) > 10%
- Profit Growth (5Y) > 12%
- EPS rising over multiple years
- Quarterly YoY growth consistent

---

## #4. Debt & Solvency
- Debt-to-Equity < 0.5 (prefer < 0.2)
- Interest Coverage > 3 (strong > 5)
- No rapid debt increase
- Debt should not outgrow revenue

---

## #5. Cash Flow Health
- Positive Operating Cash Flow every year
- OCF grows with profits
- Positive Free Cash Flow
- Healthy working capital balance
- Receivables under control

---

## #6. Valuation Checks
- PE <= industry PE
- Current PE near/below 5-year median
- EV/EBITDA lower than peers
- PB ratio reasonable (esp. for financials)
- PEG < 1 preferred

---

## #7. Shareholding Pattern
- High, stable promoter holding
- Zero promoter pledging
- FII/DII inflows positive
- Avoid sudden promoter selling

---

## #8. Red Flags to Avoid
- Declining ROE/ROCE
- Negative cash flow
- High debt + poor coverage
- Rising receivables
- Frequent equity dilution
- Auditor changes/qualified opinions
- Corporate governance issues

---

## #9. P&L Statement Checks
- Steady revenue growth
- Stable gross margin
- OPM improving
- Finance costs controlled
- Adjust for extraordinary items
- Consistent net profit trend

---

## #10. Balance Sheet Checks
- Reserves growing steadily
- Low & stable debt
- Inventory stable vs revenue
- Receivables not rising unusually
- Healthy fixed asset turnover

---

## #11. Cash Flow Statement Checks
- OCF > Net Profit (very healthy)
- Free Cash Flow positive
- Capex reasonable
- Financing not used to fund operations

---

## #12. Positive Corporate Triggers
- Capacity expansion
- New product launches
- Strategic acquisitions
- Promoter buying
- Debt reduction announcements
- Major order wins
- Strong guidance from management

---

## #13. News & Sentiment Checks
- Govt policy tailwinds
- Sector growth outlook positive
- Favorable commodity price trends
- No regulatory red flags
- Strong FII/DII participation

---

## ✅ Quick 8-Point Filter (Strong Stock Screener)
1. ROE > 15%
2. ROCE > 15%
3. Sales Growth > 10%
4. Profit Growth > 12%
5. D/E < 0.5
6. Interest Coverage > 3
7. Positive OCF
8. Zero promoter pledging

If a stock passes 6+ of these → fundamentally strong.
