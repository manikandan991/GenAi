# Entry Playbooks

## Momentum Breakout
**When**: Trend up, MACD>Signal, RSI 45–65, volume expanding.  
**Entry**: Above breakout level or on retest.  
**Stop**: Below retest low or 1.5× ATR(14).  
**Target (6 mo)**: Prior high extension or drift-based scenario.

## Mean Reversion
**When**: Range-bound, lower band tag, RSI<35 improving.  
**Entry**: On bullish candle / reclaim of range mid.  
**Stop**: Below range low.  
**Target**: Range high.

## Trend Continuation Pullback
**When**: SMA50>SMA200; pullback toward SMA20/50; MACD stays positive.  
**Entry**: Reclaim of pullback high.  
**Stop**: Below pullback low.  
**Target**: Prior swing high, then measured move.
